## For Merchants: How to Get Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [https://staging-mobipurse-app.payfusion.io/](https://staging-mobipurse-app.payfusion.io/)
2. Click on **Sign Up** or **Create Account**
3. Fill in your business information:
    - Business name
    - Email address
4. Create a secure password

### Step 2: Enable Merchant Activity

Once your account is created, you'll need to activate merchant functionality:

1. Send an email to **support@mobipurse.com** with the following information:
    - Your registered email address
    - Business name
    - Brief description of your business
    - Subject line: "Merchant Account Activation Request"
2. Our team will review your request and activate merchant features on your account
3. You'll receive a confirmation email once your merchant account is approved (typically within 1-2 business days)

### Step 3: Access Your Merchant Dashboard

After activation, log in to your account to access your merchant dashboard where you can:

- Accept payments from customers
- Debit customer wallets
- View transaction history
- Track payment status
- Generate reports
- Manage your account settings

### Step 4: Start Accepting Payments

Once your merchant account is active, you're ready to start accepting payments:

1. Navigate to your merchant dashboard
2. Generate your api-keys
3. Create Checkout Links and Redirect Your users to them
4. Monitor transactions in real-time
